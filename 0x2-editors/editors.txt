Emacs:
0 - to open a file from within Emacs press Ctrl+x  Ctrl+f .
1 - to save a file press Ctrl+x  Ctrl+s . to save as press Ctrl+x  Ctrl+w .
2 - to cut an entire line press Ctrl+k .
3 - to paste press Ctrl+y .
4 - to search forward Ctrl+s . to stop the search press Ctrl+g (this will exit the search mode).
5 - to undo most recent action press Ctrl-/  or  or Ctrl-_  . You can also use Ctrl-x u .
6 - to quit Emacs press Ctrl-x Ctrl-c .

Vim:
7 - to insert text before the cursor press 'i' . To return to normal mode after inserting text, press Esc.
8 - to delete and cut the current line press 'dd' .
9 - to paste the lines in the buffer into the text after the current line press 'p' .
10 - to undo what you just did press 'u' .
11 - to quit Vim without saving changes use the ':q!' command. 
12 - to move the cursor to the start of the current line use the '0' (zero) command.
13 - to move the cursor to the end of the line use the '$' (dollar sign) command.
